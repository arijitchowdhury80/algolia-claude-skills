## Content types

| Type | Structure | Key element |
|------|-----------|-------------|
| Joint blog post | Co-authored, both perspectives | Highlight integration value |
| Integration page | "[Partner] + Algolia: [Value]" headline | How it works + benefits |
| Joint webinar | Shared agenda, both speakers | Registration CTA |
| Solution brief | Combined solution overview | Joint customer proof |

## Co-branding rules
- Logos must have equal visual weight and appropriate spacing
- Use the logo variation that works best on the chosen background
- Neutral tone — don't favor Algolia over the partner
- Highlight the **combined** value, not just Algolia's contribution
- All co-branded content should be approved by both parties
- Follow the partner's brand guidelines for their logo and name

## Rules
- Never disparage alternatives or competitors
- Use correct partner product names (confirm with partner)
- Include perspectives from both companies
- CTAs can point to both companies' resources
- All Algolia claims must use approved stats from the messaging framework
- Sentence case for all headings
